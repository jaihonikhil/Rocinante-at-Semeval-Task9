Summary of worst-case guarantees for the considered multiwinner rules. The definitions of the rules listed in the table are provided in Section . The guarantees are functions of the committee size k . A higher value means a better guarantee, with 1 denoting optimal performance. In most cases we could only find (accurate) estimates instead of the exact values of the guarantees: the “lower” and “upper” values in the table denote that the respective guarantee is between these two bounds.
